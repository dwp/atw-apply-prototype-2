Mental health support

Access to Work mental health support is provided by Able Futures and Maximus and we will refer your details to one of these providers.

Mental health support can include things like:

  • one-to-one mental health support from a professional, outside of work
  • a support plan for your mental health
  • practical tools and techniques to help you with your mental health at work
Your employer will not be contacted about mental health support.

If you do not need any other type of support, you will need to apply directly to Able Futures (opens in a new tab) or Maximus (opens in a new tab).
